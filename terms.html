Ziv Holdings (Pty) Ltd provides an automated service that formats user-submitted information into a professional PDF Curriculum Vitae (CV). The service is delivered digitally via WhatsApp.

The cost of the service is R50.00 per generated CV. Prices are in South African Rand (ZAR). Payments are processed securely via PayFast.

This is a digital service. Upon successful payment confirmation, the final PDF document is generated and sent immediately to the WhatsApp number provided during the chat session. No physical goods are shipped.
