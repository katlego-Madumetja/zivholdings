1. Introduction

Ziv Holdings (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). This policy explains how we collect and use your data for our CV generation service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information strictly for the purpose of generating your CV:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number (for WhatsApp delivery)
  • Employment History and Education details

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Format and generate your PDF Curriculum Vitae.
  • Deliver the document to you via WhatsApp.
  • Process your payment via PayFast.

4. Payment Information

We use PayFast (Network International) to process payments. We do not store or have access to your credit card or banking details. All payment transactions occur on PayFast's secure encryption servers.

5. Data Retention

Your CV data is retained for a limited period to allow for document regeneration if needed. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
